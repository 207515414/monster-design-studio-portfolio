Questions before starting a remote production project.

What files should we send?

Send any available drawings, model files, reference images, material direction, visual references and required outputs. A clear brief reduces assumptions.

How are schedule and price determined?

They depend on project complexity, source-material completeness, required deliverables and review scope. We confirm them after reviewing the brief.

Can we begin with a paid pilot?

Yes. A small, defined paid pilot is available when it fits the project and helps both teams establish a working process.

Do you offer local approvals or stamping?

No. Monster CG provides remote visualization and technical-documentation support; it does not provide local certification, permit responsibility or professional stamping.

How is project information handled?

Public portfolio material is anonymized. Confidential project information is handled according to the agreed scope and confidentiality requirements.
