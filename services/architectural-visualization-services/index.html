Services / Architectural visualization

Architectural visualization services for design decisions and presentations.

Monster CG creates still-image visualization packages from confirmed drawings, models and references. The work helps teams explain design intent before construction.

Deliverables and inputs

Deliverables may include agreed exterior or interior views and presentation-ready image files. Useful inputs include plans, elevations, model files, material direction, camera references and an approved design brief.

Production approach

Scope, image count, review points and file formats are agreed before production. Timing depends on complexity, source material and the revision scope.

Concept visualization. The imagery does not represent a completed built project.
