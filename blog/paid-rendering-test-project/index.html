How a paid rendering test project reduces outsourcing risk.

A small, defined paid pilot gives both teams a practical way to test the brief, communication rhythm, source-file quality and review process before committing to a broader production package.

Keep the pilot specific

Choose one view or a compact drawing package, name the required inputs and agree the feedback point. The aim is to learn how the teams work together, not to obtain an unbounded free trial.
