A focused remote production studio for design teams.

Monster CG supports architects, interior designers, real estate developers and fit-out contractors with architectural visualization, 3D rendering and technical drawing production.

How we work

We join projects with a defined brief, source material, deliverables and review milestones. This keeps external production aligned with the client team's design direction and client relationship.

What we do not provide

We do not take responsibility for local permitting, architectural stamping, statutory approvals or licensed professional services. Those responsibilities remain with the appropriate locally qualified parties.

Why a paid pilot

A scoped paid pilot is a practical way to test communication, drawing standards, visual direction and review process before expanding collaboration.
